About Sarona City

Shaping Botswana's urban future on 113 hectares of master‑planned, freehold community.

Conceived and developed by Time Projects, Sarona City is a bold step towards a new urban reality. This integrated, mixed‑use development blends residential, commercial, retail, educational and recreational facilities into one cohesive community. Over 50 hectares are dedicated to open green spaces, parks and landscaped gardens, promoting wellness and environmental harmony.

With freehold ownership, fibre‑ready smart infrastructure and a "15‑minute city" philosophy, everything you need is within a short walk or cycle. Here you can truly live, work and play without compromise.

Porto Sarona City Mall

The first shopping centre within the estate, Sarona City Mall will anchor the community with a planned 7 000 m² of retail space and a curated tenant mix of 39 shops. Developed by Time Projects, it completes the live‑work‑play experience.

  • — Tenant Mix: Anchor supermarket, cinema, fashion stores, hair & beauty, pharmaceuticals, groceries, stationery, laundromat and a dedicated medical centre.
